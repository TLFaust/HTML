Our Universe

There are roughly two trillion galaxies in the known universe. Each of those galaxies are made up of billions of stars surrounded by trillions of planets. One of those planets is us. A small blue dot warmed by a yellow dwarf star. Located in the arm of relatively small galaxy locked in a cosmic dance with a Super Massive black hole that has gone on for millions if not billions of years.
